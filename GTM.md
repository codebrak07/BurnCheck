# BurnCheck AI Spend Audit — Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

Our strategy to distribute and scale **BurnCheck** as a high-conversion organic growth engine.

---

## 1. Core Target Persona
*   **Persona**: Early-to-mid stage Startup Founders, CTOs, Engineering Managers, and Operations Leads.
*   **Pain Point**: Subscription sprawl and skyrocketing monthly API/seat costs on AI tools without central visibility or structured controls.
*   **Value Proposition**: Instantly identify exact waste vectors and unlock up to 30% monthly savings in under 2 minutes, completely privately.

---

## 2. Organic Growth & Growth Loops
*   **The "Share Report" Viral Loop**:
    *   Founders generate reports showing significant savings ($500+/mo).
    *   They share the stateless link directly with their co-founders, investors, or boards to show operational efficiency.
    *   New users click the header logo to run audits on their own stacks, re-triggering the loop.
*   **Product Hunt & Startup Directories Launch**:
    *   Launch as a 100% free, privacy-first developer tool.
    *   Leverage directories (Kernal, IndieHackers, BetaList) targeting builders looking to cut software overhead.

---

## 3. High-Conversion Lead Magnets
*   **"Get Full Report" CTA**:
    *   Displays estimated values in massive monospace fonts to anchor immediate financial return.
    *   Prompts email capture to receive a structured PDF checklist.
*   **Credex Partnership Program**:
    *   Startups saving $> \$500$ monthly on API direct plans are introduced to the Credex Partner console.
    *   Offers bookable consultation slots to purchase wholesale discounted API credits, acting as the primary monetization backend.
